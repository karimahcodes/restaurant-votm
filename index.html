    Jerk Chicken

    Jamaican jerk chicken served over rice and peas or white rice with a side of seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain included. Comes with your choice of jerk sauce and/or sweet sauce.

    A savory and succulent traditionally grilled or baked chicken seasoned with a blend of Jamaican jerk spices. For a slight kick of heat, add our tangy in-house jerk sauce. For extra spice, pepper sauce is available upon request

    Curry Chicken

    Jamaican curry chicken served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Tender chicken marinated in a blend of Jamaican curry spices, slow-cooked in a rich, creamy gravy with a hint of heat

    Suggestion: traditionally served over white rice.

    Brown Stew Chicken

    Jamaican brown stew chicken is served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Tender chicken marinated in a blend of traditional Jamaican herbs and spices, then slow-cooked in a savory sauce with onions, scallions, garlic, thyme, allspice, brown sugar, and browning sauce, resulting in a rich and flavorful entree.

    Brown Stew Turkey Wing

    Stew turkey wings served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Succulent turkey wings marinated and slow-cooked in a rich and savory Jamaican-style gravy seasoned with thyme, allspice, garlic, browning and other aromatic ingredients.

    Barbecue Chicken

    Jamaican-style barbecue chicken served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Tender cuts of leg and thigh chicken marinated in a traditional Jamaican blend of thyme, garlic, scallions, and aromatic spices, then slow-cooked to perfection, resulting in a flavorful and tender dish with a hint of smoky sweetness.

    Sweet Chili Chicken

    Jamaican-style sweet chili chicken served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Tender chicken marinated in a traditional Jamaican blend of aromatic spices, then glazed with a tangy sweet and spicy chili sauce for a flavorful and tantalizing dish.

    Fried Chicken

    Jamaican-style fried chicken served over rice and peas or white rice. Comes with seasoned steamed cabbage and plantain.

    Golden brown, crispy, and highly spiced chicken, infused with a unique blend of Jamaican seasonings including garlic, ginger, thyme, and paprika, offering a decadently tender and flavorful dining experience. Top with a drizzle of sweet sauce for an extra punch of flavor.
